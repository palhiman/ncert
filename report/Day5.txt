+ Today is the "Civil Services Day". First observed on 21 April 2006 at Vigyan
Bhawan, New Delhi.
+ On this day, in 1947, the Iron Man of India, the first Home Minister of
India, Sardar Vallabhabhai Patel gave important speech at Metcalf House to
probationers of Administrative Services Officers. He said in that speech civil
servant are " the steel frame of India".

=========================================
+ Gram Sabha -> Gram Panchayats -> Panchayat Samiti (Janpad Parishad) -> Zila
Parishad (District Panchayat).

+ Gram Sabha consists of all the members of a village who are 18 y.o and above
and have right to vote.
+ Members of Gram Sabha elects Panchayat President or Sarpanch and also the
members of the Panchayats called Ward Members or panch.
+ Several Gram Panchayats collectively called Panchayat Samiti.
+ A Secretary is appointed by the government to arrange regular meeting of Gram
Panchayats, keep records of all the meetings. 
+ Panchayati Raj system is the first level of democratic government.
+ The work of Gram Panchayat has to be approved by the Gram Sabha.
+ Gram Panchayat are placed before people.
+ List of people Below Poverty Line(BPL) are to be approved by the Gram Sabha.
+ Gram Panchayat is elected for 5 years.
